Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Category: Main Course Published on: July 1, 2024

About the Recipe

Classic Spaghetti Carbonara is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. This recipe is simple yet luxurious, perfect for a comforting meal at home.

What is the Recipe?

This recipe involves cooking spaghetti and mixing it with a creamy sauce made from eggs, grated cheese, and pancetta. The heat from the pasta gently cooks the eggs, creating a rich and silky sauce.

Why This Recipe Works

The combination of the creamy egg sauce with the salty pancetta and sharp cheese makes for a flavorful and satisfying dish. The simplicity of the ingredients lets each component shine.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 50g grated Pecorino cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

How to Make

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat and cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some of the cooking water. Add the spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and quickly pour in the egg and cheese mixture, stirring constantly to create a creamy sauce.
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add some of the reserved pasta water to reach the desired consistency.
  7. Season with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.

Expert Tips

  • Use high-quality eggs and cheese for the best flavor.
  • Work quickly when adding the egg mixture to avoid scrambling the eggs.
  • Reserve enough pasta water to adjust the sauce consistency.

Recipe FAQ

  • Can I use bacon instead of pancetta? Yes, you can substitute bacon for pancetta.
  • Can I make this dish ahead of time? It's best enjoyed fresh, but you can prepare the ingredients ahead of time.

Serving

This recipe serves 2-3 people. Serve with a side salad and crusty bread for a complete meal.

Nutrition

Per serving: 450 calories, 20g fat, 45g carbohydrates, 20g protein.
